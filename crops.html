Crop Spotlight: High-Yield Corn (Maize)

Optimal Growing Profile

Soil Type: Deep, well-drained loam or silt loam.

Ideal pH Range: $6.0 - 6.8$ (Slightly acidic is best).

Key Nutrient: Nitrogen. Critical during the V6 (six-leaf) stage.

Temperature Range: $15^\circ \text{C}$ to $30^\circ \text{C}$ for healthy growth.
